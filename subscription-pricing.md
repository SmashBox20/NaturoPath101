## Business Model Rationale

### Why Freemium?
- **Accessibility**: Make health information available to all
- **Trust Building**: Let users experience value before committing
- **Viral Growth**: Free users share and refer others
- **Conversion Funnel**: ~5-10% conversion to paid tiers expected

### Why These Price Points?
- **Premium $9.99**: Comparable to other health/wellness apps (MyFitnessPal Premium, Headspace)
- **Professional $19.99**: Fraction of herbalist consultation cost ($75-150/session)
- **Lower than**: Supplement costs (users spend $30-50/month on average)
- **Psychological pricing**: Just under $10 and $20 thresholds

## FAQ - Subscription Questions

**Q: Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?**
A: Yes, you can switch at any time. When switching from monthly to annual, you'll receive a prorated credit.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**
A: Your data is retained for 90 days after cancellation. You can reactivate or export it during this period.

**Q: Can I share my subscription with family?**
A: Each subscription is for individual use. We offer a Family Plan (coming soon) for up to 5 users at a discounted rate.

**Q: Are consultations with healthcare professionals really included?**
A: Yes! Professional tier includes 2 text consultations and 1 video consultation per month. Additional consultations available for purchase.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**
A: Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on annual plans, no questions asked.

**Q: Can I try before I buy?**
A: Absolutely! Premium includes a 14-day free trial (no credit card needed), and Professional offers a 7-day trial.
