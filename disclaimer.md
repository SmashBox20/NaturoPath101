## Safety and Interactions

### Potential Risks

Natural remedies and herbal supplements can:
- Cause allergic reactions or side effects
- Interact with prescription medications
- Interfere with medical treatments or procedures
- Affect laboratory tests
- Be contraindicated in certain health conditions
- Contain contaminants or adulterants if sourced improperly

### Special Populations

Extra caution is required for:
- **Pregnant or breastfeeding women**: Many herbs are contraindicated during pregnancy or lactation
- **Children**: Dosing and safety profiles differ for pediatric use
- **Elderly individuals**: May have increased sensitivity to herbs and higher risk of interactions
- **People with chronic conditions**: Such as liver disease, kidney disease, autoimmune disorders, heart conditions
- **Those taking medications**: Risk of herb-drug interactions
- **Pre-surgical patients**: Many herbs affect bleeding, anesthesia, or wound healing

**ALWAYS consult with a qualified healthcare provider before using any herbal remedy or supplement if you fall into any of these categories.**
